Membership Eligibility:
1. Residence: Membership at Kimwanyi SACCO is open to individuals residing within Kimwanyi Town.

2.Age Requirement: Members must be at least 18 years old to be eligible for membership. This ensures that individuals have reached the legal age to engage in financial transactions and benefit from our services.

3.Community Engagement: We encourage individuals who are actively involved in community activities and have a strong commitment to the betterment of our local area to join Kimwanyi SACCO. Our SACCO values community engagement and aims to build a strong network of members working towards a common goal.

4.Affinity to SACCO's Values: We seek individuals who align with our mission, vision, and values, and are committed to the principles of financial responsibility, cooperation, and mutual support. Members should share our dedication to community development and financial inclusion.

5.Other Requirements: Additional specific eligibility criteria or documentation may be required, such as proof of residency, identification documents, and any applicable membership fees.
